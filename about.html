About Tensorgami

Mathematical Writing

Purpose

Tensorgami is Henry Shin's public archive of mathematical writing. It contains expository notes, proof notes, problem sets, and Lean formalizations.

The emphasis is on arguments that are readable, inspectable, and worth preserving. Some entries include Lean source files and automated checks, so the exposition can be compared with a formally verified version.

Henry studied mathematics at Harvard with Richard Taylor.

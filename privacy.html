Privacy

Privacy

A short note on analytics and contact data.

Analytics

Minimal analytics

Tensorgami uses minimal self-hosted analytics for pageviews and PDF-link clicks. It does not use advertising trackers, analytics cookies, or public visitor counters.

The site script sends the event type, host, path, page title, referrer, and browser timezone to a Cloudflare Worker endpoint maintained for Tensorgami. This is used to understand whether pages and PDFs are being reached. The script does not set cookies or create a persistent visitor identifier.

Cloudflare may process ordinary request metadata as part of delivering the endpoint. Email sent to the listed contact address is handled as ordinary email.

Opt Out

Browser setting

To turn off Tensorgami analytics in this browser, use this opt-out link. It stores tensorgamiAnalyticsOptOut = "1" in local storage.

To restore analytics in this browser, use this opt-in link. Browser do-not-track signals are also respected.
